STORY BEHIND
Best Selling Kit & Future Plans
Our vision is simple. Create the largest and the most comprehensive design kit available for both designing and integration. It all started in 2016 with a release of very first version of Dashboard UI Kit. It quickly became a bestseller both on Gumroad and UI8 platforms. With that in mind, we decided to take it few steps further and create an ultimate source for any designer and developer and that's how new product Dashboard UI Kit 3.0 come to life.
‍
Our goal is to deliver a kit consisting of 40 screens developed in React (and HTML & CSS) and 100 screens firstly in Sketch and then in other designing tools in the future. We are going to take this process closer to you and open a full roadmap so you can see what's coming your way.
