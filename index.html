STORY BEHIND

Best Selling Kit & Future Plans

Our vision is simple. Create the largest and the most comprehensive design kit available for both designing and integration. It all started two and half years ago with a release of very first version of Dashboard UI Kit. It quickly became a bestseller both on Gumroad and UI8 platforms. With that in mind, we decided to take it few steps further and create an ultimate source for any designer and developer.
‍
Our goal is to deliver a kit consisting of 60 screens developed in React (and HTML & CSS) and 100 screens in Sketch and other designing tools by Q2 next year. We are going to take this process closer to you and open a full roadmap so you can see what's coming your way.
